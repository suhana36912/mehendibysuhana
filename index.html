About Us

Welcome to Mehendi by Suhana, where every design is a blend of tradition, elegance, and creativity.
I'm Suhana, a professional Mehendi artist based in Udupi, with over 8 years of experience in adorning hands and hearts through the timeless art of henna. Whether it's a grand bridal celebration, a joyful baby shower, or a festive party,I specialize in Bridal Mehendi, Arabic designs, Party Mehendi, and custom creations for every occasion.What sets my work apart is attention to detail and a deep passion for personalizing every design to suit your story. I believe Mehendi is more than just a tradition — it's an experience, a memory etched in elegance. Offering home services and destination bookings, I ensure that your Mehendi journey is as smooth and special as the designs themselves. Let's make your special moments truly unforgettable — one beautiful pattern at a time.
